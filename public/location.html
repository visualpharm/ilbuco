About Cariló

Cariló is a coastal town in Buenos Aires Province, Argentina, known for its pine forests, sand dunes, and upscale villas. Founded in the early 20th century by Héctor Manuel Guerrero, the town has sandy, unpaved roads and houses built among mature trees.
