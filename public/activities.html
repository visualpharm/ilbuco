Things to Do

Discover the many activities and dining options available in and around Cariló during your stay at Il Buco.

Sports & Recreation

  • Cariló Tennis Club – Clay courts, padel, football, small gym
  • Centro Integral de Entrenamiento (CIE) – Full indoor gym with modern equipment
  • Aire Libre, Libre – Free 24-hour outdoor calisthenics gym
  • Cabalgatas Dos Montes – Horse riding through dunes & forest
  • Motorrad Cariló – Quad riding and fatbike rental for beach and forest exploration

Beach Activities

  • Swimming and sunbathing on Cariló's pristine beaches
  • Beach volleyball courts available for public use
  • Surfing and bodyboarding (equipment rental available nearby)
  • Beach yoga classes (seasonal)
  • Fishing excursions can be arranged

Dining & Coffee

Restaurants

  • La Cabrera – Parrilla with lots of extras
  • Don Benito – Upscale dining experience
  • De Mi Campo – Fresh salads and healthy options
  • La Parrillita – Basic but good Argentine grill
  • Il Gatto Nero – Award-winning Napoletan pizza (Valeria del Mar)
  • Enri's Cariló – Burgers (Impeke in Valeria also good)
  • Masse – Always open, reliable option

Cafés & Bakeries

  • Panettone – Coffee, baked goods, avocado toast
  • Café del Bosque – Forest setting with outdoor seating
  • Dulce Cariló – Pastries and desserts

Shopping & Culture

  • Cariló Shopping Center – Boutiques and specialty shops
  • Local artisan market (weekends during high season)
  • Art galleries featuring local artists
  • Seasonal cultural events and performances
